HOW PEOPLE CAN MAINTAIN SELF-LOVE

Tips to Attain Self-Love





Stop comparing yourself to others

Weâ€™re socialised to be competitive, so comparing ourselves to others is natural. But remember that every person has a unique traits that makes us stands out to one another. Focus on what you got and maximize your full potential in that way you will feel more about yourself.

Allow yourself to make mistakes

We're taught from a young age that "no one is perfect" and that "everyone makes mistakes." However, when you grow older, you'll feel more pressure to never fail. Give yourself a break! Make mistakes so you can learn from them and improve. Accept your history. You're still growing and evolving from who you were to who you are now and will be in the future.

Get through your worries.

Fear, like wavering, is a real and human emotion. Understand your doubts rather than dismissing them. This beneficial exercise will significantly improve your mental health. Examining and assessing your fears will help you gain insight and uncover problems that have been causing you anxiety. As a result, some if not all of your anxiety could be relieved.

Take advantage of any opportunity
that comes your way.

The next big step in your life will never be flawless in terms of timing. Even if the setup isn't perfect, you shouldn't let that stop you from achieving your goals and dreams. Instead, take advantage of the opportunity because it will never come again.
